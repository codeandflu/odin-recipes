French Toast

From france with love, or not sure

French toast is a classic breakfast dish made by dipping slices of bread in a mixture of beaten eggs, milk, and a touch of cinnamon or vanilla, then frying them until golden and crispy. The result is a warm, slightly crispy exterior with a soft, custardy interior. Often served with a drizzle of maple syrup, fresh fruit, powdered sugar, or a sprinkle of cinnamon, French toast is a comforting, versatile dish that can be enjoyed sweet or savory. It’s the perfect way to transform simple ingredients into a delicious and satisfying meal.! Find the full recipe below:

Ingredients

  • Bread – 4 slices (preferably thick-cut, like brioche, challah, or white bread)
  • Eggs – 2 large
  • Milk – 1/2 cup (or use cream for a richer texture)
  • Vanilla extract – 1/2 tsp (optional)
  • Ground cinnamon – 1/4 tsp (optional)
  • Sugar – 1 tbsp (optional, for sweetness)
  • Salt – a pinch
  • Butter – for frying
  • Maple syrup – for serving (optional)
  • Powdered sugar – for dusting (optional)
  • Fresh fruit – for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla, cinnamon, sugar, and salt in a shallow bowl.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat, add butter.
  3. Dip bread slices into egg mixture, coating both sides.
  4. Cook on the skillet for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown.
  5. Serve with maple syrup, powdered sugar, and fresh fruit.
